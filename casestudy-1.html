Meet LearningRoots
Learning Root is a UK-based publishing brand specializing in creative Islamic educational resources for children. From storybooks to games, Learning Root inspires young minds with products that instill fun, faith, and foundational values. Despite a strong mission, the company needed expert website optimization to reach its full potential in conversions and engagement.
The Challenge
Learning Root faced a roadblock in scaling its e-commerce growth. Their website had solid traffic but was not converting at its full potential. The challenges included:
  • Low Conversion Rates: Visitors weren’t moving through the purchase funnel efficiently.
  • User Experience (UX) Gaps: Key sections of the site had friction points that hindered engagement.
  • Experimentation: No structured A/B testing had been conducted to optimize page performance.
The company sought a partner to redesign its conversion strategy and provide actionable insights backed by data to maximize online revenue.
The Approch

We crafted a robust, full-service Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) plan for Learning Root, leveraging user behavior analysis and strategic enhancements:

Product Page Optimization
Drop Day Urgency
Cross-Selling
Product Page Optimization

Streamlined Navigation for Effortless Product Exploration

We optimized Learning Root's product pages to enhance the browsing experience, making it seamless for parents to navigate through various educational products. Each product category was strategically displayed for easy comparison and quick access.
Drop Day Urgency

Maximizing Sales with Strategic Drop Day Messaging

To drive engagement, we implemented urgency tactics that made Learning Root’s site feel active and in-demand. On drop days, notifications were displayed to show which popular educational products were selling fast, motivating parents to make timely purchases.
Cross-Selling

Boosting Revenue with Strategic Cross-Selling

By introducing targeted cross-sells across key product pages, we successfully increased average order value (AOV) per customer. This strategy was refined and validated through A/B testing, showing a significant boost in conversion rates and overall revenue per visitor.
