Tracking Audit and Data Governance

Our tracking audit and compliance services provide ongoing monitoring and reporting of data quality, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements and best practices.

Why Tracking and audit ?

Accurate tracking data plays a crucial role in making informed decisions. However, it is susceptible to distortion caused by configuration errors, inconsistencies, or technical issues. Such distortions can introduce errors in the analysis process and hinder effective decision-making.

Ensuring data reliability and addressing these challenges necessitate specialized expertise and a deep understanding of tracking tools. Many organizations may encounter limitations in terms of internal resources or knowledge to ensure the accuracy and usability of their data.

With D-SPACE, you receive comprehensive tracking audits and a guarantee of data reliability. We assist you in optimizing and safeguarding the quality of your data, enabling informed decision-making and enhancing the effectiveness of your marketing efforts.

The benefits of conducting a tracking and reliability audit include:
  • Precise Identification and Resolution of Tracking Errors and Inconsistencies
  • Enhanced Data Quality and Accuracy
  • Improved Performance and Informed Decision Making
  • Discovery of Opportunities for Growth and Improvement
  • Minimized Risk of Erroneous Data
  • Expertise in Utilizing Tracking Tools for Comprehensive Audits
  • Ongoing Monitoring to Ensure Data Reliability
  • Time and Efficiency Savings in Data Analysis Processes

What is Our Approach?

Our comprehensive digital analytics audit process ensures that all parameters are considered, regardless of the digital tools you use. The end result of our data audit is the provision of accurate and complete data sets that support your organization's needs.

Our digital analytics audit process includes the following steps:
  1. Review of Current Analytics Setup:

    We will thoroughly examine your existing analytics tools and configuration to ensure that you are collecting the appropriate data and utilizing the most suitable tools for your requirements.

  2. Data Collection and Reporting Assessment:

    We will assess your data collection and reporting processes to identify any gaps or inaccuracies, ensuring that your data is reliable and comprehensive.

  3. Recommendations for Improvement:

    Based on our findings, we will provide recommendations to enhance your analytics setup and optimize your data collection and reporting processes, empowering you to make data-driven decisions effectively.

  4. Implementation Support:

    We are dedicated to providing support throughout the implementation phase, assisting you in implementing our recommendations and optimizing your analytics setup according to your specific needs.

Once we have thoroughly examined every aspect of your analytics setup, you will receive a comprehensive report summarizing our findings in each area. Alongside our recommendations, we will provide detailed explanations of why implementing or testing them would be beneficial for your analytics performance.
