Transforming E-Commerce Marketing with AI BQML: A Real-Life Success Story

September 29, 2024
By
  Ravi Kukadiya
Learn how Instant BigQuery Machine Learning (iBQML) can transform your marketing strategy through audience segmentation and predictive analytics in this real-life example.

What is iBQML and Why Does it Matter?

iBQML, built on BigQuery Machine Learning (BQML), integrates directly with Google Analytics, empowering marketers to create machine learning models that predict customer behavior without writing complex code. With just a few clicks, iBQML enhances audience targeting and remarketing efforts by analyzing and predicting customer behavior.

Real-Life Example: Optimizing E-commerce Campaigns with iBQML

An e-commerce business selling premium home furniture used iBQML to tackle two challenges:

  • High customer acquisition costs: They were spending too much on ads to attract new customers.
  • Low repeat customer rate: Although they were getting initial sales, few customers returned for a second purchase.

Applying iBQML to Solve the Problem

Here's how the marketing team leveraged iBQML to reduce customer acquisition costs and increase repeat purchases:

  1. Audience Segmentation with Propensity Modeling: iBQML analyzed data from Google Analytics, such as time spent on the website, pages visited, and purchase history. It identified customers most likely to make a repeat purchase, creating a high-propensity audience segment.
  2. Targeted Remarketing Campaigns: The high-propensity segment was exported to Google Ads for remarketing campaigns. This allowed the team to offer discounts to customers most likely to buy again, ensuring more efficient ad spend.
  3. Predicting New Customer Acquisition: iBQML also identified new potential customers who had similar behaviors to high-value repeat customers. These were targeted in acquisition campaigns.

The Results

The furniture company saw impressive results after using iBQML:

  • 30% reduction in customer acquisition costs: The company attracted more qualified leads while spending less.
  • 15% increase in repeat purchases: Remarketing campaigns successfully re-engaged past customers.
  • 25% improvement in ROAS: The focus on high-potential customers improved the return on ad spend.
“BQML empowers marketers to predict customer behavior without complex case, enhancing targeting and remarketing through Google Analytics integration. ”

Key Takeaways from This Example

This example demonstrates how iBQML can help businesses unlock machine learning’s potential. Here are the main takeaways:

  • Efficient Audience Targeting: iBQML's ability to segment audiences based on predicted behavior leads to more efficient ad spend and better campaign performance.
  • Actionable Insights Without Coding: iBQML is a no-code solution that empowers marketers to act on insights quickly.
  • Reduced Time-to-Insight: The entire pipeline was set up in just 30 minutes, allowing the team to start optimizing right away.
  • Scalability and Flexibility: Whether for acquisition or retention, iBQML can scale to meet various business needs.

Conclusion: Why Marketers Should Embrace iBQML

Machine learning is shaping the future of marketing, and iBQML is one of the easiest ways to get there. Its ability to provide powerful insights with minimal technical expertise makes it a valuable asset for any business. By integrating iBQML, the furniture company reduced costs, increased customer retention, and optimized ad spend. For any business looking to take its marketing to the next level, iBQML is a must-have tool.
