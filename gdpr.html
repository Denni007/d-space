Description

The management of cookies and tracking is a major issue for companies, with strict regulations such as the GDPR. Companies must ensure that they respect confidentiality rules to avoid penalties and maintain user trust.

The complexity of regulations and CMPs can make it difficult for companies to comply. Without the right tools and expertise, compliance can be time-consuming and expensive.

Here are the steps involved in our process:

  • Assess your current cookie usage. This includes identifying the types of cookies you use, how you use them, and who you share them with.
  • Define your cookie consent requirements. This includes deciding what types of consent you need to collect, how you will collect it, and how you will store it.
  • Choose a cookie consent solution. There are a number of cookie consent solutions available, so you will need to choose one that meets your specific needs.
  • Implement your cookie consent solution. This includes configuring the solution to meet your requirements and deploying it on your website or app.
  • Monitor your cookie consent solution. This includes ensuring that it is working properly and that you are collecting the required consent.
  • Stay up-to-date with regulatory changes. Cookie laws are constantly changing, so you will need to make sure that your cookie consent solution is compliant with the latest regulations.
